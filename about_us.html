Grandmaster's Chess Academy

Our Vision

At Grandmaster's Academy, we believe chess is more than a game—it's a tool for developing critical thinking, foresight, and discipline. Founded by national masters, our mission is to make high-level chess education accessible to everyone, fostering a new generation of strategic thinkers both on and off the board.

Our Journey

2018 — The Idea Was Born

A group of national chess masters came together with a shared dream: to make elite chess education available to every curious mind, regardless of background

2021 — Tournament Successes

Our students began winning national and international youth tournaments, validating our belief in personalized, strategic coaching.

2024 — 1,000+ Students Strong

From beginners to rising stars, our academy grew into a vibrant learning community—proving that with the right guidance, anyone can master the game.
